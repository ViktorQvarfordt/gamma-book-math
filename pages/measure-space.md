# Measure space

:::definition[Measure space]
A measure space is a [tuple]() $(X, \mathcal{A}, \mu)$ where

* $X$ is a [set]()
* $\mathcal{A}$ is a [$\sigma$-algebra](sigma-algebra) on $X$
* $\mu$ is a [measure]() on $(X, \mathcal{A})$
:::
